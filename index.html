Introduction

Hope Schwartz is a talented graphic designer born and raised in Kearney, Nebraska. She attended and graduated from University of Nebraska in Kearney in May of 2021 with her Bachelors Degree in Visual Communication and Design. Hope recently joined the work force and is starting her journey towards pursuing her ultimate goal of becoming Art Director.

Oil Paintings

One of Hope's favorite ways to express herself and her artwork is through oil paintings. Here is a self portrait that Hope made in one of her painting classes at UNK. When asked about this self portrait, she said "I like to take regular photos, and then alter them using a digital medium." She also likes to distort the images once she uploads them to her software.

Accomplishments

Through the start of the pandemic, Hope picked up on roller-skating. Throughout the past year, she has continued to learn how to roller-skate and also how to do tricks. Hope has recently graduated from UNK. She expressed how much this meant to her, and how proud she was of herself for completing the tough courses through the art program. She is also proud to have gotten a job within her line of work.
